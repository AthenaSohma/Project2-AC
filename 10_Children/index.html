It follows a couple, Burt and Vicky, who stumble upon a deserted town in rural Nebraska where all the adults have been gruesomely murdered and the children rule under the leadership of a sinister figure named Isaac. The children worship a malevolent entity called "He Who Walks Behind the Rows" and sacrifice adults to it. Burt and Vicky must fight for their lives against the murderous children and the supernatural force that controls them. The story explores themes of religious fanaticism, the corrupting influence of power, and the primal fear of children turning against adults.
