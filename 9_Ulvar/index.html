In the future, the discovery of Universal Nodes leads to the realization that the universe may be a simulation. Despite risks, attempts are made to deactivate these Nodes. When successful, parts of the universe vanish, revealing the simulated nature of reality. Exploration expands as more Nodes are deactivated, leading to the discovery of planets with artificial life. However, deactivating a fifth Node unexpectedly leaves behind ruins, challenging the understanding of reality. Questions arise about the true nature of existence and the consequences of tampering with it.
